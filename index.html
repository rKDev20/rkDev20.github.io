Is it secure?

Yes absolutely! The app doesn't send any of your data to its own server. The username and password are stored locally on your device and is used whenever you update your attendance. This can't be accessed by anyone, not even you untill and unless your phone is rooted. Also there are no sensitive permission required to run the app. Storage permission may be asked which is not compulsory for the app to work. So why it is even there? Ok, I will tell you. Go to hamburger menu->SAP(under social)->login to sap and try downloading any thing from sap portal(such as admit card, semester grade report, fees receipt, etc). For this purpose app will need the storage permission to save those files onto your phone. It is only then when the app will ask for that permission else just deny the permission and the app will work amazingly!

Many people tend to believe that the app is stealing their data. But wait, and think what could be done with that data?Sell it? Lets assume that such data can be sold, but thats possible only when the data is in bulk. Even if everyone in KIIT starts using the app, and IF your data was mined, not much money can be generated.

The motive of the app was personal usage at the beginning. The idea to put this up for others to use came very later and the purpose was only to share the work and help others.

Its completely on you to decide whether to use the app or not!
